# ☕ Cafe Sales Insights: Brewing Data into Decisions! 🚀

Welcome to my Cafe Analytics project! I took a massive dataset of coffee shop transactions (140,000+ rows!) and turned it into a clear, visual story about what people drink, when they drink it, and where they spend the most.

Because every great cup of coffee starts with great data! ☕✨

### 🧐 What’s Under the Hood?
I analyzed sales from multiple locations like **Astoria**, **Lower Manhattan**, and **Hell's Kitchen**.
- **Data Wrangling:** Transformed raw transaction logs into clean, structured data.
- **Pivot Wizardry:** Calculated total sales ($450k+) and identified top-selling products (Spoiler: Coffee is king! 👑).
- **Time Analysis:** Identified peak hours and busy days to help with store staffing.
- **Dynamic Visuals:** Created charts to track product performance across categories like Bakery, Tea, and Drinking Chocolate.

### 🛠️ Prerequisites & Setup
To explore this dashboard:
1. **Microsoft Excel (2019+):** To experience the full interactivity.
2. **Love for Caffeine:** Highly recommended while browsing the charts. ☕

### ⚙️ Technical Skills Showcased
- **Advanced Excel:** VLOOKUPs, Complex Pivot Tables, and Data Modeling.
- **Business Intelligence:** Analyzing Sales Trends and Unit Price impacts.
- **Data Visualization:** Interactive Slicers and Dashboard Design.
